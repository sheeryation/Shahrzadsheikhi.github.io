# Research Interests & Projects

* **Black Hole Physics:** Determining mass and spin of the black hole inside **GRB 220101A** using Fermi/LAT.
* **X-Ray Binaries:** Identifying **1RXS J180408.9-342058** as an Ultra-Compact X-ray Binary (UCXB).
* **Fast Radio Bursts (FRBs):** Investigating expectations for FRBs in black hole-neutron star binary systems.
* **Stellar Modeling:** Experienced in simulating stellar evolution using the **MESA** platform.
